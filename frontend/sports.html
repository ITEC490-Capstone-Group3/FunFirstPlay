⚽️ Soccer ⚽️

Soccer (also called football around the world) is a thrilling, fast-paced team sport where two teams of 11 players compete to score goals by kicking the ball into the opposing teams' net. It's the world's most popular sport, loved by millions for its simplicity, excitement, and teamwork.

How To Play

⚽️  Kickoff: Start at the center of the
field with a pass.

⚽️  Passing & Dribbling: Move the ball between teammates or dribble it down the field.

⚽️  Scoring Goals: Kick the ball into the opposing team’s net to score.

⚽️  Defense: Block opponents from reaching your goal.

⚽️  Goalkeeper: Protect the goal; the only
player allowed to use hands (inside the penalty area).

⚽️  Timing: The game is typically 90 minutes long (two halves of 45 minutes).

What You Need

⚽️  Soccer Ball (Size 5 for Adults)

⚽️  Soccer Cleats (For grass or turf)

⚽️  Shin Guards (to protect your legs)

⚽️  Athletic Clothes 

⚽️  Long Socks (to cover the shin guards)

⚽️ Suggestions: Water bottle, training cones, goalie gloves

Basic Rules

⚽️  No Hands: Only the goalkeeper can touchthe ball with their hands.

⚽️  Offside Rule: Attackers must stay behind the second-to-last defender when receiving the ball.

⚽️  Throw-Ins: When the ball goes out of
bounds over the sideline, the other team throws it back into play.

⚽️  Fouls & Free Kicks: Breaking rules like tripping earns free kicks or penalties for the other team.

⚽️  Corner Kicks & Goal Kicks: Different ways to restart play when the ball crosses the goal lines.

🏈 Football 🏈

Football (American football) is a high-energy team sport where two teams compete to move the ball down the field and score points by getting the ball into the opponent's end zone. It's known for its strategy, athleticism, and powerful plays, and it's one of the most popular sports in the U.S.






How To Play

🏈 Kickoff: One team kicks the ball to the
other to start the game.

🏈  Downs System: Offense has 4 tries (downs) to
move the ball 10 yards forward.

🏈  Defense: Stop the opposing team from
advancing and try to take possession of the ball.

🏈  Special Teams: Handle kickoffs, punts, and
field goal attempts.
🏈  Game Length: 4 quarters, each lasting 15
minutes (with halftime in the middle).

🏈 Scoring Points:

Touchdown (6 points): Carry or catch the
ball in the opponent’s end zone.

Field Goal (3 points): Kick the ball
through the goalposts.

Extra Point (1 or 2 points): Kick after a
touchdown or run/pass for 2 points.

What You Need

🏈  Football (official size or junior size
for kids)

🏈  Helmet (with a face mask and chin
strap)

🏈  Football Jersey and Pants (usually padded)

🏈 Cleats (shoes with proper traction for
grass or turf)

🏈 Gloves (optional, for better grip when
catching)

🏈  Shoulder Pads
🏈  Mouthguard

Basic Rules

🏈  Tackling: Players can tackle the ball carrier to stop progress.

🏈  Offside: Players must not cross the line of scrimmage before the ball is snapped.

🏈  Fumbles and Interceptions: Losing possession of the ball gives the other team a chance to take over.

🏈  Penalties: Breaking rules can cost a team yardage or even possession.

🏀 Basketball 🏀 

Basketball is a fast-paced team sport where two teams of five players each try to score points by shooting a ball through the opponent's hoop. It’s played on a rectangular court with a hoop at each end and is loved for its energy, skills, and quick scoring.

How To Play

🏀  Tip-Off: The game starts with a jump
ball at center court.

🏀  Dribbling: Players move the ball by
bouncing it while walking or running.

🏀  Passing: Move the ball between teammates to find open shots.

🏀  Defense: Block, steal, or rebound the
ball to prevent the opponent from scoring.

🏀  Game Length: 4 quarters of 12 minutes each (NBA) or different depending on league

🏀  Shooting Score: 
2 Points: Regular field goal inside the
3-point line.
3 Points: Field goal beyond the 3-point
line.
1 Point: Free throws after certain
fouls.

What you Need

🏀 Basketball (size 7 for men, size 6 for
women, size 5 for kids)

🏀  Basketball Hoop (with a 10-foot-high rim)

🏀  Basketball Shoes 

🏀  Athletic Clothing 

🏀  Suggestions: Water bottle, knee pads,
wristbands

Basic Rules

🏀 No Traveling: Players must dribble when moving their feet.

🏀  No Double Dribble: Can't stop dribbling and then start again.

🏀  Fouls: Physical contact like hitting or pushing is not allowed.

🏀 Shot Clock: Teams must attempt a shot within 24 seconds.

🏀  Out of Bounds: Ball goes to the other team if it goes out
